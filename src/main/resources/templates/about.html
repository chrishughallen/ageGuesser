Welcome to the age guessing app!

Age Guesser is a simple application that lets you guess other users ages, and see what age people think you are.

It's free to play, and only requires an email address, your birthday, and a picture to get started.

You can guess users ages without signing up, however, if you do sign up, you'll see how accurate you are at guessing, and how old you really look.

Give it a try!
